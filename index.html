our story.

4 friends, 1 formerly cancerous, ready to change the world for the better.
Rowan was diagnosed with Ewings Sarcoma, a rare bone cancer, in December of 2004 when he was 13 years old. After over a year of chemotherapy, surgeries and fighting, he celebrated remission. after a celebration that lasted a little more than a year, spots were found during a regular x-ray check up. Rowan's cancer had come back in his lungs. Disappointed, but not disheartened, he began new treatments. Surgery, chemotherapy and this time an anologus stem-cell transplant that push his body to the brink of death.
