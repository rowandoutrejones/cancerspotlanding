Cancer Spot connecting everyone in the world affected by cancer.

connect and support

Cancer Spot provides a mobile platform for fighters, survivors and supporters of cancer to connect and support each other 24/7.

connecting

Users will be connected based off shimilar characteristics such as type of cancer, age and location.

This way a 35 year old in Montana with Leukemia will have the opportunity to get advice and share stories with a 33 year old Leukemia survivor in Los Angeles.

messaging

After connecting, mutual support will take place through mobile messaging.
